Player's Option: Skills & Powers (second printing) Errata

Skills & Powers Errata and Clarifications effective 9 February 1996

This is the official Errata List complied by Creative Director Steve Winter for the second printing of the S&P rulebook. Since this is only a reprint (not a redesign or a complete re-edit) most of the fixes are small: fixing typos, correcting mess-up charts, matching page references with the correct page numbers, and so on. These corrections will clear up a lot of the confusion that the first printing of S&P generated, ans this file also replaces the FAQFile for S&P. I hope you find information this useful and thanks again for all the support you folks have shown S&P in the nine months or so. Good gaming!

- Dale Donovan
