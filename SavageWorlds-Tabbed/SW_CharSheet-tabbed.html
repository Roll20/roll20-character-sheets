API: Initiative Edges iDealInit API Script

Initiative Edges must be stored in a comma separated list in an Attribute named InitEdges. (e.g. Qu,LH)

The Edge shorthand is as follows:

  • Qui = Quick
  • LH = Level Headed
  • ILH = Improved Level Headed
  • WCE = Any Joker Activated Wild Card Edge

Melee Attack Options

Attack OptionWhy do it?
Called Shot You can deal additional damage to your opponent, but penalties apply to Fighting.
  • Head/Vitals: -4 to hit, +4 Damage
  • Limb: -2 to hit, +2 Damage
  • Small Target: -4 to hit, Special Damage
  • Tiny Target: -6 to hit, Special Damage
DisarmRenders your opponent weaponless, requires a called shot.
Draw & AttackGet a jab in quickly. -2 to Fighting.
The DropYou're catching someone completely unawares (GM's discretion). +4 to Fighting and Damage.
Finishing Move (insta-kill)The foe is utterly helpless and needs to be taken out.
FrenzyGet to two attacks with the same weapon. Requires an Edge.
Gang-upStrength in numbers. +1 to Fighting/per Ally, to a max +4
GrapplingYou don't want to kill your opponent, just subdue him.
Improvised WeaponYou need a weapon, and it's all that's available. -1 to Fighting and Parry
Multi-actionYou're surrounded or just need to put your foe(s) down ASAP. -2 to all rolls per additional action.
Non-lethal DamageYou don't want to kill your opponent, just subdue her. If using an edged weapon, suffer a -1 to the Fighting roll.
Off-hand AttackYour dominant hand is otherwise occupied and you need to do something. -2 to Fighting
PushYou need to reposition your opponent. Opposed Strength rolls. Can move your foe up to 2".
Rapid AttackYou're desperate and you've done the math... Allows you to make 3 Fighting attacks. -4 to all Fighting rolls, and uses a single Wild Die. You suffer -2 to Parry until next your next action
Touch attackYou're using something that just needs to touch your opponent, but not necessarily physically hurt them. +2 to Fighting
Use two weaponsYou took the Edge and want to get in two attacks with no multi-action penalty (off-hand penalty still applies)
Unarmed DefenderYour opponent has no weapon and suffers a -2 to Parry.
Wild AttackYou need to put the enemy down, so give it all you've got. +2 to Fighting and Damage, -2 to Parry.

Ranged Attack Options

Attack OptionWhy do it?
AimImprove your chances of hitting your opponent. +2 to Shooting/Throwing (requires a round to aim).
Called Shot You can deal additional damage to your opponent, but penalties apply to Shooting/Throwing.
  • Head/Vitals: -4 to hit, +4 Damage
  • Limb: -2 to hit, +2 Damage
  • Small Target: -4 to hit, Special Damage
  • Tiny Target: -6 to hit, Special Damage
DisarmRenders your opponent weaponless, requires a called shot.
Double-tapShoot like a cop! +1 to Shooting, expends 2 bullets
Draw & AttackGet a jab in quickly. -2 to Fighting.
The DropYou're catching someone completely unawares (GM's discretion). +4 to Fighting and Damage.
Finishing Move (insta-kill)The foe is utterly helpless and needs to be taken out.
Firing BlindYou know someone is "there," but you can't see them. -4 to Shooting/Throwing
Full AutoYou need to fill the air with lead, fast. Roll 3 Shooting dice and 1 Wild Die, all rolls are at -2.
Improvised WeaponYou need a weapon, and it's all that's available. -1 to Throwing and Parry
Multi-actionYou're surrounded or just need to put your foe(s) down ASAP. -2 to all rolls per additional action.
Off-hand AttackYour dominant hand is otherwise occupied and you need to do something. -2 to Shooting/Throwing
Rapid Attack/Fanning the HammerYou're desperate and you've done the math... Allows you to make 6 Shooting attacks. -4 to all Fighting rolls, and uses a single Wild Die. You suffer -2 to Parry until next your next action
Suppressive FireYou need to keep heads down. Requires Full-Auto. Spray a Medium Burst Template.
Three Round BurstNeed to improve your accuracy to hit your opponent. +2 to Shooting, expends 3 bullets
Use two weaponsYou took the Edge and want to get in two attacks with no multi-action penalty (off-hand penalty still applies)
Attack OptionWhy do it?
Cover You're getting attack and don't have enough armoror you just don't want to get hit.
  • Light: attacker suffers -1 to hit
  • Medium: attacker suffers -2 to hit
  • Heavy: attacker suffers -4 to hit
  • Total: attacker suffers -6 to hit
DefendWant to withdraw from combat, or just don't want to get hit in a melee fight? +2 to Parry and can move half pace.
DisarmRenders your opponent weaponless, requires a called shot.
Full DefenseYou really can't afford to get hit, and just need to buy some time for the cavalry to arrive... Make a Fighting roll +2, and this is your new Parryyour Parry can not get worse, because of this manuever.
Illumination Your opponent can see you too easilymake it dark!
  • Dim: attacker suffers -1 to hit
  • Dark: attacker suffers -2 to hit
  • Pitch Dark: attacker suffers -4 to hit
If you suffer these penalties, try turning on the lights...
Prone Make yourself harder to hit when being shot at: in most instances functions as medium cover.
Just don't go prone in Melee...-2 to Parry and Fighting.
WithdrawTake your chances and get the heck out of dodge. All adjacent foes get a free attack, but you might get out unscathed and live to fight another day.
Attack OptionWhy do it?
Movement Don't just stand there...move around.
  • Crawling: move 2"/turn and counts as prone when being fired upon.
  • Crouching: move at half pace, ranged attacks suffer -1.
  • Walking: move your full pace as a free action.
  • Running: move your full pace +1d6 as an action.
  • Jumping: jump horizontally 1" (from dead stop), or 2" with a run. A success Strength roll adds an addition 1".
  • Go Prone: fall prone at any time during your action. Getting up costs 2" of movement.
Test of WillsYou're quick witted or scarier than your foe. Use a Taunt or Intimidation skill. Success gives +2 to your next action against the opponent, and a Raise scores a Shaken result in addition.
TricksYour foe isn't as nimble, or smart as you. Use a Smarts or Agility Trick. Success and the foe suffers -2 to Parry until their next action, on a Raise your opponent is also Shaken.
