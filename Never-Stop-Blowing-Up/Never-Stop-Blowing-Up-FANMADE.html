Never Stop Blowing Up is a tabletop roleplaying game based on the Kids on Bikes system which pulls in mechanics from other systems like Roll for Shoes, lovingly home-brewed by Brennan Lee Mulligan and the team at Dimension 20. It is designed to put you into the world of action movie heroes where stakes are high, the guns are loaded, and literally everyone is hot.

TURBO TOKENS

Ã—

Turbo Tokens are awarded for failing any skill or ability check or gifted by the GM for inspired roleplaying decisions.Turbo Tokens can be spent by the player at any time in 1 of 3 ways:


  • 1. A player may use tokens at a 1:1 ratio to succeed or otherwise modify a skill or ability check for themselves. Turbo Tokens may be used to make up the difference between the maximum roll on a die and what was rolled to Blow Up a roll. For example: a 3 is rolled on a d12, you can use 9 Turbo Tokens to Blow Up the roll.
  • 2. A player may use tokens at a 2:1 ratio to help another party member succeed a skill or ability check, if they are present in a scene when the check occurs.
  • 3. To purchase skills/ability increases individually or Group Abilities in between sessions.
