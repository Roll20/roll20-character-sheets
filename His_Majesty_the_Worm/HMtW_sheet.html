Weapons

Axe: If a target is killed, apply same attack to another foe in range
Flail: Always win ties (i.e., against foes with shields)
Dagger: Ignores armor when a foe is rooted, tripped, or disarmed
Hammer/Mace: Deal 2 wounds when attack is 2x+ foe's initiative
Sword: Deal 2 wounds on a successful riposte
Polearm: Play a riposte as an interrupt
Bow: May aim as an action and add the aim value to the next attack
Crossbow: Always ignores armor, reload with miscellaneous action
Shield: Play any card face-down as a miscellaneous action, replacing initiative; as a defense wins ties that are attack vs. initiative
