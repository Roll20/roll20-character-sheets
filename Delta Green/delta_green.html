rolltype Define the type of roll used, if:

0: Skill or Stat roll (general modifier and difficulty)

1: Combat Roll (general modifiers and attack related modifiers)

2: Special Combat Roll (general modifier and Combat Dependant roll)

specialnc Define the type of special non combat roll used, if:

0: Sleeplessness roll: check if you can fall asleep after reaching breaking point or acute episode (modified by sleeping pills and alchool)

2: Permanent Injury: when you get HP<=2 different possible Permanent Injuries
