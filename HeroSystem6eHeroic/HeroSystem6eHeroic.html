Standard Maneuvers

Block

1/2 Phase

OCV: +0

DCV: +0

Block HTH attacks with successful OCV vs. OCV roll. Additional blocks suffer cumulative -2 OCV penalties. Gives priority over standard initiative order on next attack. May Abort next phase to use.

6E2 57

Disarm

1/2 Phase

OCV: -2

DCV: +0

Disarm opponent if attack roll and a STR vs STR contest succeed. Additional OCV modifiers may apply (0 to -3 for HTH and more for ranged).

Brace for +2 RMod at 1/2 DCV.

Set adds +1 OCV to ranged attacks.

6E2 60

Dodge

1/2 Phase

OCV: —

DCV: +3

Increase DCV against non-AoE attacks until next phase. Counts as attack action. May Abort next phase to use.

6E2 61

Grab

1/2 Phase

OCV: -1*

DCV: -2*

Grab opponent with two limbs with successful attack roll, who may attempt escape. May follow with Squeeze, Slam, or Throw. *Success causes both characters to be at 1/2 DCV and each suffers various OCV penalties.

6E2 62

Grab By

1/2 Phase

OCV: -3

DCV: -4

Grab object while making a Full Move. Adds velocity/10 to STR for the purpose of making Strength checks.

Velocity:

6E2 67

Haymaker

1/2 Phase*

OCV: +0

DCV: -5

Add +4 DC to any attack (with GM discretion) at the cost of -5 DCV. Attack lands at the end of the following segment. Can be aborted. Fails if the target moves.

Brace for +2 RMod at 1/2 DCV.

Set adds +1 OCV to ranged attacks.

6E2 68

Move By

1/2 Phase

OCV: -2

DCV: -2

Attack while making a Full Move past opponent. Strength is halved, but maneuver adds (velocity/10) DCs. Attacker or weapon takes 1/3 of this damage.

Velocity:

6E2 70

Move Through

1/2 Phase

OCV: -v/10

DCV: -3

Collide with opponent at the end of a Full or Half Move. Does full STR or weapon at half STR plus (velocity/6) DCs. Attacker takes 1/2 damage or full damage if attack does no Knockback or Knockdown.

Velocity:

6E2 70

Multiple Attack

Full Phase

OCV: varies

DCV: 1/2

Attack multiple times. Each attack after the first suffers a cumulative -2 OCV. Miss ends sequence.

Brace for +2 RMod at 1/2 DCV.

Set adds +1 OCV to ranged attacks.

6E2 73

Set

Full Phase

OCV: +1*

DCV: +0

Spend a phase to add +1 OCV to ranged attacks against selected target.

6E2 79

Shove

1/2 Phase

OCV: -1

DCV: -1

Push target up to 1 meter per 5 STR used, which target may resist. A collision does STR damage, but is limited to object PD plus BODY.

DC Limit:

6E2 79

Strike

1/2 Phase

OCV: +0

DCV: +0

Standard attack, which does damage by STR or weapon.

Brace for +2 RMod at 1/2 DCV.

Set adds +1 OCV to ranged attacks.

6E2 80

Throw

1/2 Phase

OCV: +0

DCV: +0

Throw object or character. STR damage limited to object PD plus BODY.

DC Limit:

Brace for +2 RMod at 1/2 DCV.

Set adds +1 OCV to ranged attacks.

6E2 80

Trip

1/2 Phase

OCV: -1

DCV: -2

Knock target down prone (half DCV) with a successful attack roll. A moving target takes (velocity/20)d6 damage.

Velocity:
