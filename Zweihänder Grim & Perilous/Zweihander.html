Zweihander Grim & Perilous Character Sheet has been Updated
• This character sheet has had it's attributes updated to follow a consistent naming convention. When you opened the sheet all of the old values of the attributes were migrated to the new attributes. These new attributes all have a html "Title" so that when you hover your mouse over the input field you will see the name of the attribute that is storing your inputs.
• During the update process the data in the Select Menus for weapon skill and bonus damage type was changed to a different format. You will need to reselect the weapon skill and bonus damage type for each weapon. Simply toggle the select to None and then back to the proper skill, and to None and then back to the proper bonus damage type.
• During the update process some roll button names were changed to follow a consistant naming convention. You will need to change the name of button calls in macros and or redrag the buttons to your macro bar. Button names can all be seen by hovering the mouse over the button.
• More information can be found on the Roll20 Wiki, link in the footer below. (This banner can be hidden in the Footer options section at the bottom of the character sheet.)
