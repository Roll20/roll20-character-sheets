Level Up
Each level you gain a new domain card of that tier (you may trade out an existing one) and make two advancement picks from the list below. Choosing Proficiency +1 ★★ or Multiclass ★★ uses both picks for that level.
Tier 2 — Levels 2–4
Achievement (L2): Proficiency +1 · gain a new Experience at +2
Increase 2 unmarked traits of your choice by +1. Update trait values at the top of the sheet. Mark the traits you increased. Up to 3× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Hit Point slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Stress slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 Evasion. 1× this tier.
Increase two of your Experiences by +1 each. Update Experiences above. 1× this tier.
Gain one additional domain card of this tier. You may trade out an existing card. 1× this tier.
Tier 3 — Levels 5–7
Achievement (L5): Proficiency +1 · new Experience +2 · clear tier marks
Increase 2 unmarked traits of your choice by +1. Update trait values at the top of the sheet. Mark the traits you increased. Up to 3× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Hit Point slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Stress slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 Evasion. 1× this tier.
Increase two of your Experiences by +1 each. Update Experiences above. 1× this tier.
Gain one additional domain card of this tier. You may trade out an existing card. 1× this tier.
Take an upgraded subclass card. Then cross out the multiclass option for this tier. 1× this tier.
Increase your Proficiency by +1. Uses both picks for this level.
Multiclass: Choose an additional class for your character, then cross out an unused 'Take an upgraded subclass card' and the other multiclass option on this sheet. Uses both picks for this level.
Tier 4 — Levels 8–10
Achievement (L8): Proficiency +1 · new Experience +2 · clear tier marks
Increase 2 unmarked traits of your choice by +1. Update trait values at the top of the sheet. Mark the traits you increased. Up to 3× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Hit Point slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 maximum Stress slot. Up to 2× this tier.
Gain +1 Evasion. 1× this tier.
Increase two of your Experiences by +1 each. Update Experiences above. 1× this tier.
Gain one additional domain card of this tier. You may trade out an existing card. 1× this tier.
Take an upgraded subclass card. Then cross out the multiclass option for this tier. 1× this tier.
Increase your Proficiency by +1. Uses both picks for this level.
Multiclass: Choose an additional class for your character, then cross out an unused 'Take an upgraded subclass card' and the other multiclass option on this sheet. Uses both picks for this level.

Level-Up Features
Armored
When your companion takes damage, you can mark one of your Armor Slots instead of marking one of their Stress. May be chosen once.
Aware
Your companion gains a permanent +2 bonus to their Evasion. May be chosen up to three times.
Bonded
When you mark your last Hit Point, your companion rushes to your side to comfort you. Roll a number of d6s equal to the unmarked Stress slots they have and mark them. If any roll a 6, your companion helps you up. Clear your last Hit Point and return to the scene. May be chosen once.
Creature Comfort
Once per rest, when you take time during a quiet moment to give your companion companionship and attention, you can gain a Hope or you can both clear a Stress. May be chosen once.
Learned
Your companion gains a permanent +1 bonus to a Companion Experience of your choice. May be chosen up to three times.
Light in the Dark
Use this as an additional Hope slot your character can mark. May be chosen once.
Resilient
Your companion gains an additional Stress slot. May be chosen up to three times.
Vicious
Increase your companion's damage dice or range by one step (d6 to d8, Close to Far, etc.). May be chosen up to three times.
Vehicle Training
Your companion gains the ability to perform one of the Vehicle Crew Positions except Commander. Each time you choose Vehicle Training choose a new Crew Position the companion can perform. May be chosen up to three times.
