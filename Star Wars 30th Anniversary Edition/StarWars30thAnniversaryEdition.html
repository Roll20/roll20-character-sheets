Dice Codes
Dice codes must be written as xD or xD+y. For example, 2D and 2D+2 are valid. Damage codes for weapons can use str (Strength) and ctrl (Control for lightsabers), for example str+2D.

Important! For a dice code to update you need to tab away from the field before you press a roll button.

Armor Codes
Armor code must be either a value with a plus sign, for example +1; or a normal dice code, example 1D.

Wound Status
Write one or two letters:
  • H = Healthy
  • S = Stunned
  • W = Wounded
  • I = Incapacitated
  • M = Mortally Wounded
W, I and M will reduce all dice codes with 1D. H and S will revert back to normal dice codes.
