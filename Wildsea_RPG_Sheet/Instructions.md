# Wildsea RPG Sheet (Custom Roll20 Sheet)

This sheet is intended for use as a **Custom** character sheet in a Roll20 game.

## Use it in Roll20 (Custom Sheet)
1. In Roll20, open your game.
2. Click **Settings** (top right) â†’ **Game Settings**.
3. Under **Character Sheet Template**, select **Custom**.
4. Copy/paste the contents of `sheet.html` into the **HTML Layout** box.
5. Copy/paste the contents of `sheet.css` into the **CSS Styling** box.
6. Click **Save Changes**.
7. Go back to the VTT, refresh the page, then create/open a character to see the sheet.

## Notes
- Repeating sections must follow Roll20 rules (no underscores in repeating section names).
- This sheet uses only HTML/CSS (no sheetworkers).
