Hail, devoted warrior of the Emperor!

With considerable help from the Adeptus Mechanicus, this character sheet is now translatable. You may head to the Roll20 wiki to know how you can help the Imperium and translate this sheet in your language.

However, the Inquisition told me how impure and complex this sheet is, and they think the source code has been exposed to the Warp for too long, for it is considered tainted by HTML experts.
Thus, I, brother Baleine82 suggest to be the vessel of the Inquisition in cleaning this sheet so everyone would enjoy it (any help is welcome).
I already opened a subject on the forum (https://app.roll20.net/forum/post/9565800/deathwatch-sheet-update-translation-and-certainly-more-to-come) where you may tell me what you like and what you don't about this character sheet.

Your insights will lead this sheet to something everyone will enjoy.

Oh, by the way, please check your Navigation skills in the first tab of the sheet. I'm sorry about that, but you need to put back how well you where proficient in them (hint: fresh space marines are trained in Navigation:Surface from their very start). There's nothing I can do about that, it was a bug in the previous version.


May the Imperium prevails in all its battles!
