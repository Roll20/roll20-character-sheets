This page is a tool for creating your character, it will try to guide you through the various stages of the process. You will need acces to a copy of the rulebook for most of this.

Use the checkboxes to display adittional information.

About Homeworld

First select your Homeworld

Your GM will tell you which worlds are appropriate for your campagin, enter the name of the world below. The drop-down list contains the worlds of the Reformation Coalition and adjusts homeworld values accordingly.


If you wish to use another world as homeworld select other and roll random homeworld details according to the corebook or Find the UWP (Universal World Profile) of the world. You can use travellermap.com and/or check with your GM.

Enter the data belov. Note that Traveller uses and "extended hexadecimal" numbersystem where two digit numbers are represented with letters. A=10, B=11 etc. This form is not able to decifer these letters so enter 12 if the UWP states C etc. This does not apply for the first digit, "Starport", which is a letter-code and should entered as a letter.

About Attributes

Now we roll for attributes. Roll seven times and disregar the lowes roll. You are free to chose which roll is placed at which attribute.

Modifiers are based on the UWP of your homeworld and final values are sendt to the character sheet. Note that it is possible to increase attribute values during charter creation or gameplay, but no higher than 2 points. With the maximum homeworld modifer of 2 points the absolute max value for any attribute is 15.

If your attribute total is less than 36 you may add points freely until the total is 36.

Note that with age these numbers might be modified again.


Homeworld effects

The sheet automatically adjusts attributes according to the following rules:

If Homeworld size is 2 or less - subtract 2 from Strenght and Constitution (< 0.3 G)

If Homeworld size is 3 or 4 - subrtact 1 from Strenght and Constitution (> 0.3 G < 0.5 G)

If Homeworld size is C (12) to F (15) add 1 to Strenght and subtract 1 from Constitution (> 1.5 G <

If Homweorld gravity is > 2G add 2 to Strength< and subtract 2 from Constitution

If Homweorld atmosphere is 0 add 1 to Agility (Vacuum)

If Homeworld atmosphere is 2, 4 or 9 subtract 1 from Constitution (Tainted)

If Homeworld population is 5 or lower subtract 1 from Charisma

If Homeworld population is 9 or higher add 1 to Charisma

If Homeworld tech-level is 3 or lower subtract 2 from Education and add 1 to Constitution

If Homeworld tech-level is between 9 and G (13) add 1 to Education

If Homeworld tech-level is E (14) or higher add 2 to Education

About Age effects

Characters start choosing careers at 17 and each term lasts 4 years. Starting age is the number of careers multiplied by 4 pluss 17.


As the characters age certain ablities might be affected. . If the roll is lower than the attribute being checked one point is lost. Use the table below to see which ablities are affected.

Effects of age might be combated using physical development and/or Anagathics. These are detailed in the core book.

A few more things

Initiative is determined once, depending on a roll and the types of careers you have chosen. If you have any military careers you can roll , if not roll rounded down, but counting 0.5 as 1. Add initiative bonuses from "other effects", but civilian bonues only apply for the civillian roll option.


Choose a base weight. The Core book uses 65kg for women and 80kg for men, but you are free to choose whatever you feel like. This number will be adjustet by your strength and agility.
