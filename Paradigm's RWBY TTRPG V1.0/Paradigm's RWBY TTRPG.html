ATTRIBUTES
STR
Strength
Your ability to perform physical tasks when trying to physically exert and overpower an obstacle uses Strength. Strength is one of the primary attributes used for those who possess great melee prowess.
END
Endurance
Your ability to take punishment after your Aura has been broken and how long you can perform any task that requires physical exertion. Withstanding hazardous environmental and physical trauma is also a key aspect of those with high Endurance. Additionally, Endurance determines a Character’s Health Points.
AGI
Agility
Your ability to move quickly, precisely, and with poise. Tasks that require accurate and precise movement or use of delicate maneuvers require Agility. Agility is one of the primary attributes used by those excelling in ranged combat. It also affects your movement bonus.
PER
Perception
Your ability to defend yourself relies on your Perception. Perception is used to spot weaknesses or incoming attacks to better defend oneself as well as hone in on your own intuition about others.
WIL
Willpower
Your ability to Influence others and manipulate your Semblance. You also have a knack for understanding how to approach aggressive and delicate social interactions.
DIS
Discipline
Your ability to be studious and keep calm during the heat of battle. Characters with high Discipline exhibit extreme form and finesse when wielding Dust. Individuals with high Discipline are highly skilled with Dust without applying it to a weapon.
MOVE
Movement
The amount of distance you can travel with one Action.
The default distance you move is 15ft. Gain +5ft for every 4 AGI.

RESOURCES
HP
HP is equal to your END + 1. How much damage a Character can withstand without their Aura before becoming Defeated. Health Points are commonly referred to as HP. When your HP is 0, your Character is considered Defeated. Only HP restoration can bring a Character out of the Defeated state.
/ 
AURA
Aura is equal to your WIL + DIS + 2. The physical manifestation of a Character’s soul. Every Huntsman and Huntress is trained in its capabilities. Aura acts as a barrier to protect your body from damage. When your Aura is 0 your Character cannot use their Semblance; any damage inflicted will be directed to your HP.
/ 
CAPACITY
The amount of ammunition left in a magazine, emergency supplies, or easily accessible Dust a character has at the current moment. Capacity is expended when a Character performs a ranged attack, Dust Skill Check or other Combat Action. When your Capacity is 0 your Character cannot perform Actions that require Capacity to be performed. Capacity may be restored to its maximum by using a Minor Action called Reload.
/ 
ROC
A special resource that provides numerous options. Rule of Cool may be awarded by GM for a lavish and cool description of an attack, a partner attack, team attack, or anything at all they feel deserves it. The maximum RoC you can possess is 5.
/ 
DESTINY
A special Resource that is always available.
ARMOR
Some Characters wear extra Armor, or a Faunus may have natural Armor. Reduce any damage that would be directed to your HP by 1 for every point of Armor you possess to a minimum of 1. The maximum Armor you may ever have is equal to 1 + 1 for every 4 points of Strength you possess.
/ 
BARRIER
An augmentation to your Aura is Barrier. The maximum Barrier you may possess is equal to your Level/2 + 1.
/ 
SUPPLY
Representing the amount of backup ammunition, Dust, or other essentials the Character has on their person but not readily available. Supplies are expended by 1 when a Character performs a Reload. When your Supplies are 0 your Character cannot perform a Reload.

SIGNATURE ACTION
Signature Action
Your custom Signature Action that is unique to your character! You may perform a Signature Action once per Combat Encounter.
Signature Action
Perform your Signature Action! Signature Attacks may only be performed Once per Combat Encounter. Signature Attacks cannot be combined with any Rule of Cool option that inflicts a Detrimental Status Effect.
Signature Action Damage
Perform damage from your Signature Action! Inflicts (RoC Unlock x 1D6) + Level/2 in Damage.
Signature Type
Signature Action Type utilizes your Strength for Melee, Agility for Ranged, and Discipline for Characters with the Dust Weaver Specialization.
RoC Unlock
The amount of RoC expended to perform this Signature Action.
Primary Attribute
The secondary Attribute that is utilized while performing your Signature Action.
Secondary Attribute
The secondary Attribute that is utilized while performing your Signature Action.
Area of Effect
Your Target and Close range Adversaries and Allies to the Target are affected by your Signature Action.
If you are utilizing the Anti-Material Weaponry, Cleaving, Collateral Damage, High Explosive, or Returning Weapon Specializations, the effect changes to the following:
Perform Signature Action Damage with Advantage instead if any of the previous Weapon Specializations apply.
Beneficial
Beneficial (WIL)
Critical Threshold
Reduce the Critical Threshold of Adversaries affected by your Signature Action by 5 while calculating Signature Action Damage.
Defense Bypass
Ignore Barrier and Armor of your Target while calculating Damage from your Signature Action. Destroy half of any Barrier and Armor of your Target after your Signature Action resolves.
Detrimental
Detrimental (PER)
Disrupt
While Targeting 1 or more Adversaries, you may move them up to 15ft in any direction and decrease their position by 1 in the Initiative Order.
Dust Bolster
You and Allies affected apply the strongest Status condition if applicable from any Dust infused to your Signature Action. Add +5 to your Signature Action for the purposes of calculating any Dust inflicted Status Effect that inflicts Damage when dividing by 10.
Fortify
You may distribute a total amount of Armor equal to your Signature Action / 10 among yourself and Allies affected.
Initiative Change
You and Allies affected may Increase or decrease their position in the Initiative Order up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action.
Multi Target
You may Target Entities within range of your Signature Action up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action.
Outmaneuver
You and Allies affected may immediately perform a Move Action with a Capacity Move that does not expend a Minor Action.
Range
Increase the range of your Signature Action up to the value of the Rule of Cool required to use your Signature Action.
Recycle
Restore Capacity equal to your Signature Action / 10.
Safeguard
You may distribute a total amount of Barrier equal to your Signature Action / 10 among yourself and Allies affected.
