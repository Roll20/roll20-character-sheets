ATTRIBUTES
STR
Raw force to physically overpower obstacles and a primary Attribute for Characters that excel in Melee combat.
END
A Huntsman's capability to weather physical trauma and environmental hazards, and the determining Attribute for HP.
AGI
Quick, precise, and with a flash of flair, a primary Attribute for Characters that excel in Ranged combat.
PER
A sharp intuition and keen eye keep a Huntsman from being caught unaware, and the primary Attribute for Defense Reactions.
WIL
The inherent magnetism of a powerful personality opens endless doors, and the primary Attribute for unleashing a Semblance.
DIS
Studious with a hefty dose of self-control, and the primary Attribute for creatively utilizing Dust.
MOVE
Movement = 15 ft + 5 ft for every 4 points of AGI.
Your ability to move around a battlefield at a certain speed. Your Movement determines the distance of your Move and the enhanced Capacity Move while in Initiative Order.

RESOURCES
HP
Vehicle Maximum HP is determined by its Sizing and Specializations. When a Vehicle's HP is reduced to 0 it is considered Defeated, and is neither operational nor provides a function.
/ 
ARMOR
Decrease any Damage that would be directed to the Vehicle HP by 1 for every point of Armor the Vehicle possesses (Minimum of 1). Vehicle Maximum Armor is determined by its Sizing and Specializations. Vehicles always start with their maximum Armor (GM Discretion).
Once per Defense Reaction, you may expend 1 Armor to re-roll your Resist Defense Reaction.
/ 
BARRIER
Vehicle Maximum Barrier is equal to half of its starting Maximum HP. Vehicles never start with Barrier unless specified by an effect.
/ 
VEHICLE CAPACITY
Vehicle Maximum Capacity is determined by its size and Specializations. Increase Vehicle Capacity equal to half of the Vehicle's Maximum Vehicle Capacity at the start of the Pilot's Turn or Scene. Vehicle Capacity, specific to Vehicles, can only be used to perform Actions while Piloting a Vehicle.
/ 
VEHICLE SUPPLY
Vehicle Supply is determined by its size and Specializations. Vehicle Supply may be expended to increase 1 Vehicle's Ranged Weapon Form's Weapon Capacity to its maximum by expending 1 Vehicle Capacity and 1 Vehicle Supply, or a Passenger may expend 1 Minor Action and 1 Vehicle Supply.
/ 
SHARED RESOURCES
ROC
A special resource available in Combat Encounters. A GM may award RoC for a lavish and cool description of an Attack, a partner Attack, a team Attack, or anything at all they feel deserves it.
/ 
DESTINY
A tide-turning Resource that is gained when fate aligns throughout play! You can replace any value of a D10 impacting your Character with the value of your Destiny.

DETRIMENTAL STATUS EFFECTS
Bewildered
Decrease Attacks and Abilities by 2 until the end of your next Round's Turn. If an instance of Bewildered would be inflicted while an Entity is already Bewildered, upgrade Bewildered with Blinded.
Blinded
Decrease Attacks and Abilities by 4 until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Broken
Decrease Defense, Resist Defense Reactions, Parry Defense Reactions, and Dodge Defense Reactions by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Burning
Take Damage equal to the stacks of Burning you possess at the end of your Turn. Burning persists until removed. Burning stacks may be decreased by performing a relevant Action (GM Discretion). Decrease Burning stacks equal to (Action ÷ 10).
Chilled
MOV is decreased by 5 ft until the end of your next Round's Turn. Decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1 at the end of the Combat Round. If an instance of Chilled would be inflicted while an Entity is already Chilled, upgrade Chilled with Frostbitten.
Concussed
Empower and Infuse cannot be used. Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Dust Defense Reactions, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 4 until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Disoriented
Empower cannot be used. Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Dust Defense Reactions, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 2 until the end of your next Round's Turn. If an instance of Disoriented would be Inflicted while an Entity is already Disoriented, upgrade Disoriented with Concussed.
Distracted
You may not perform Boost Actions. Decrease Skill Checks by 2 except Dust Skill Checks and Semblance Skill Checks until the end of your next Round's Turn.
If the afflicted Entity uses NPC Attributes, it instead decreases the Damage inflicted from Attacks and Abilities by 1 (Minimum 1).
If an instance of Distracted would be inflicted while an Entity is already Distracted, upgrade Distracted with Pressured.
Exhausted
Persevere cannot be used. Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Reactions by 4 until the end of your next Turn.
Exposed
Critical Damage triggers on a Mini Crit. Lose Exposed after Critical Damage is inflicted.
Frostbitten
MOV is decreased by 10 ft until the end of your next Round's Turn. Decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1 at the end of the Combat Round. If an Entity is already Frostbitten, Chilled is not inflicted but inflicts Damage equal to (Action ÷ 10).
Grappled
A Grappled Entity cannot perform the Move Action. The first Action performed each Combat Round against an Entity afflicted with Grappled gains an instance of Advantage.
Hindered
Decrease Attacks, Abilities, and Skill Checks by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Impaired
Decrease Special Defense, Dust Defense Reactions, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Immobilized
Move Actions cannot be performed until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Pressured
You may not perform Boost, Dust Swap or Reload Actions. Decrease all Skill Checks by 4 except Dust Skill Checks
and Semblance Skill Checks until the end of your next Round's Turn.
If the afflicted Entity uses NPC Attributes, instead decrease it instead decreases the Damage inflicted from Attacks and Abilities by 2 (Minimum 1).
Staggered
Lose 1 Action from your Action Pool at the start of your next Turn and then lose Staggered. If an instance of Staggered would be applied while an Entity is already Staggered, upgrade Staggered with Stunned.
Strained
The next time you perform a Move, expend all stacks of Strained and take damage equal to the number of stacks. Strained stacks may be removed by performing a First Aid Skill Check. Remove Strained stacks equal to a (First Aid Skill Check ÷ 10.)
Stunned
Lose 1 Major Action and 1 Minor Action at the start of your Turn and then lose Stunned.
Weakened
Persevere cannot be used. Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Reactions by 2 until the end of your next Round's Turn. If an instance of Weakened would be applied while an Entity is already Weakened, upgrade Weakened with Exhausted.
BENEFICIAL STATUS EFFECTS
Charged
You may perform a free Boost with any Ability, Attack, Defense Reaction, or Skill Check. Lose Charged after this effect has resolved.
Cover
Decrease Damage received from Ranged Attacks by 2. Lose Cover if you are no longer located within Adjacent Range to the Entity providing Cover. If Cover is provided by an object such as a wall and the Damage would exceed 2, that Cover would be considered destroyed or no longer usable.
Diffraction
The next Damage greater than 1 inflicted to you is halved before performing any other Damage reduction effects. Lose Diffraction after this effect has resolved.
Extinguish
Nullify the next Detrimental Status Effect that would be inflicted. If multiple Detrimental Status Effects would be applied simultaneously, you may select which one to nullify. Lose Extinguish after this effect has resolved.
Fleet Footed
Increase your MOV by 10 ft. Lose Fleet Footed at the end of the Combat Round you gained it, and you may increase or decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1.
Fury
While performing Damage, increase your Damage equal to the stacks of Fury you possess. Lose all stacks of Fury after this effect has resolved.
Harmonized
Your next Empower is performed with Advantage. Lose Harmonized after this effect has resolved.
Keen Eye
While performing an Attack, increase your Attack equal to the stacks of Keen Eye you possess. Lose all stacks of Keen Eye after this effect has resolved.
Regeneration
If Regeneration would be applied, add stacks of Regeneration equal to (Action ÷ 10). At the end of your Turn you must either expend 1 stack of Regeneration and restore 1 HP or expend 3 stacks of Regeneration and restore 1 Aura. If your HP and Aura are at their maximum values, expend 1 stack of Regeneration.
Reinforced
Armor can decrease Damage to 0 instead of a minimum of 1. Your next Persevere is performed with Advantage. Lose Reinforced when Damage is decreased to 0 from an Action or a Persevere Action is performed.

RESOURCES
HP
Health Points = END + 1
How much Damage a Character can withstand without Aura before becoming Defeated. When your HP is 0, your Character is considered Defeated. Only restoring HP can bring a Character out of the Defeated state.
/ 
AURA
Aura = WIL + DIS + 2
The physical manifestation of a Character's soul, utilized by Huntsmen to protect their bodies from peril. When your Aura is 0, your Character cannot use or benefit from their Semblance; any Damage inflicted will be directed to your HP. Beware! Some Attacks may bypass Aura!
/ 
SUPPLY
Representing the amount of backup ammunition, Dust, or other essentials the Character has on their person but not readily available. Supply is expended by 1 when a Character performs a Reload. When your Supply is 0, your Character cannot perform a Reload.
/ 
ROC
A special resource available in Combat Encounters. A GM may award RoC for a lavish and cool description of an Attack, a partner Attack, a team Attack, or anything at all they feel deserves it.
/ 
DESTINY
A tide-turning Resource that is gained when fate aligns throughout play! You can replace any value of a D10 impacting your Character with the value of your Destiny.
ARMOR
Max Armor = 1 + 1 for every 4 points of STR
A defensive resource typically gained from a Specialization or Asset that offers moderate protection from harm. Decrease any Damage that would be directed to your HP by 1 for every point of Armor you possess to a minimum of 1. Beware! Some Attacks may bypass or even destroy Armor!
/ 
BARRIER
Barrier Max Barrier = 1 + (LVL ÷ 2)
An additional layer of protection generated from Dust, a Specialization, or an Asset. Barrier protects you from all incoming Damage and is always decreased first before Aura and HP.
/ 
DEFENSE REACTIONS
Dodge
AGI + PER
When you successfully perform a Dodge Defense Reaction and exceed the Attack by 5 or more, you may perform a Move in any direction after all Attacks from Entities in that Initiative Order position resolve.
+
Dust
DIS + PER
You must possess Dust and at least 1 Capacity to perform this Defense Reaction. When you successfully perform a Dust Defense Reaction and exceed the Attack by 5 or more, you may gain the Support effect based on the type of Dust you have Active by expending 1 Capacity.
+
Parry
STR + PER
When you successfully perform a Parry Defense Reaction and exceed the Attack by 5 or more, you may inflict X Damage to your Attacker, where X is your PER. You cannot use a Parry Defense Reaction against Ranged Attacks.
+
Resist
END + PER
When you successfully perform a Resist Defense Reaction and exceed the Attack by 5 or more, remove 1 Detrimental Status Effect except Pressured, Distracted, Grappled, or Poisoned from yourself after the Attack has resolved. When you fail a Resist Defense Reaction, you may take the Damage to your HP instead of your Aura and may use this effect multiple times per Turn. A Resist Defense Reaction may utilize the Persevere Action.
+
Semblance
WIL + PER
When you successfully perform a Semblance Defense Reaction and exceed the Attack by 5 or more, you may perform your Semblance if applicable, using your Semblance Defense Reaction as your Semblance Skill Check. You may only perform your Semblance this way once per Combat Round. A Semblance Defense Reaction may utilize the Empower Action. A Semblance Defense Reaction cannot be used if your Aura is 0.

Take Damage equal to the stacks of Bleeding you possess at the end of your Turn. Bleeding persists until removed. Bleeding may only be applied when an Action with this effect is directed to HP or (Action ÷ 10 ) is greater than your Aura. If Bleeding would be applied, inflict stacks of Bleeding equal to (Action ÷ 10.)
Bleeding stacks may be decreased by performing a First Aid Skill Check. Decrease Bleeding stacks equal to (First Aid Skill Check ÷ 10).
Blinded
Decrease Attacks and Abilities by 4 until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Broken
Decrease Defense, Resist Defense Reactions, Parry Defense Reactions, and Dodge Defense Reactions by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Burning
Take Damage equal to the stacks of Burning you possess at the end of your Turn. Burning persists until removed. Burning stacks may be decreased by performing a relevant Action (GM Discretion). Decrease Burning stacks equal to (Action ÷ 10).
Chilled
MOV is decreased by 5 ft until the end of your next Round's Turn. Decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1 at the end of the Combat Round. If an instance of Chilled would be inflicted while an Entity is already Chilled, upgrade Chilled with Frostbitten.
Concussed
Empower and Infuse cannot be used. Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Dust Defense Reactions, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 4 until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Confused
While an Action is performed that results in failure, take Damage equal to (Action ÷ 10) until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Disoriented
Empower cannot be used. Decrease Special Defense, Dust Skill Checks, Dust Defense Reactions, Semblance Skill Checks, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 2 until the end of your next Round's Turn. If an instance of Disoriented would be Inflicted while an Entity is already Disoriented, upgrade Disoriented with Concussed.
Distracted
You may not perform Boost Actions. Decrease Skill Checks by 2 except Dust Skill Checks and Semblance Skill Checks until the end of your next Round's Turn.
If the afflicted Entity uses NPC Attributes, it instead decreases the Damage inflicted from Attacks and Abilities by 1 (Minimum 1).
If an instance of Distracted would be inflicted while an Entity is already Distracted, upgrade Distracted with Pressured.
Exhausted
Persevere cannot be used. Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Reactions by 4 until the end of your next Turn.
Exposed
Critical Damage triggers on a Mini Crit. Lose Exposed after Critical Damage is inflicted.
Frostbitten
MOV is decreased by 10 ft until the end of your next Round's Turn. Decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1 at the end of the Combat Round. If an Entity is already Frostbitten, Chilled is not inflicted but inflicts Damage equal to (Action ÷ 10).
Hindered
Decrease Attacks, Abilities, and Skill Checks by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Grappled
A Grappled Entity cannot perform the Move Action. The first Action performed each Combat Round against an Entity afflicted with Grappled gains an instance of Advantage.
Impaired
Decrease Special Defense, Dust Defense Reactions, and Semblance Defense Reactions by 5 until the end of the Combat Encounter.
Immobilized
Move Actions cannot be performed until the end of your next Round's Turn.
Poisoned
Poisoned may only be applied when an Action with this effect is directed to HP or if remaining Aura is less than (Action ÷ 10). Decrease all Aura and HP restoration by the number of Poisoned stacks you possess. Take Damage equal to half the stacks of Poisoned each time a new or upgraded Detrimental Status Effect is afflicted to you. Poisoned persists until removed.
Poisoned stacks may be removed by performing a First Aid Skill Check. Remove Poisoned stacks equal to a (First Aid Skill Check ÷ 10.)
Pressured
You may not perform Boost, Dust Swap or Reload Actions. Decrease all Skill Checks by 4 except Dust Skill Checks
and Semblance Skill Checks until the end of your next Round's Turn.
If the afflicted Entity uses NPC Attributes, instead decrease it instead decreases the Damage inflicted from Attacks and Abilities by 2 (Minimum 1).
Shaken
Your next Attack, Skill Check, and Defense Reaction are each performed with Disadvantage. You cannot gain any instance of Advantage, and do not benefit from Mini Crit effects until the end of your next Turn.
Staggered
Lose 1 Action from your Action Pool at the start of your next Turn and then lose Staggered. If an instance of Staggered would be applied while an Entity is already Staggered, upgrade Staggered with Stunned.
Strained
The next time you perform a Move, expend all stacks of Strained and take damage equal to the number of stacks. Strained stacks may be removed by performing a First Aid Skill Check. Remove Strained stacks equal to a (First Aid Skill Check ÷ 10.)
Stunned
Lose 1 Major Action and 1 Minor Action at the start of your Turn and then lose Stunned.
Weakened
Persevere cannot be used. Decrease Defense, Dodge, Parry, and Resist Defense Reactions by 2 until the end of your next Round's Turn. If an instance of Weakened would be applied while an Entity is already Weakened, upgrade Weakened with Exhausted.
BENEFICIAL STATUS EFFECTS
Charged
You may perform a free Boost with any Ability, Attack, Defense Reaction, or Skill Check. Lose Charged after this effect has resolved.
Cover
Decrease Damage received from Ranged Attacks by 2. Lose Cover if you are no longer located within Adjacent Range to the Entity providing Cover. If Cover is provided by an object such as a wall and the Damage would exceed 2, that Cover would be considered destroyed or no longer usable.
Diffraction
The next Damage greater than 1 inflicted to you is halved before performing any other Damage reduction effects. Lose Diffraction after this effect has resolved.
Extinguish
Nullify the next Detrimental Status Effect that would be inflicted. If multiple Detrimental Status Effects would be applied simultaneously, you may select which one to nullify. Lose Extinguish after this effect has resolved.
Fleet Footed
Increase your MOV by 10 ft. Lose Fleet Footed at the end of the Combat Round you gained it, and you may increase or decrease your position in the Initiative Order by 1.
Fury
While performing Damage, increase your Damage equal to the stacks of Fury you possess. Lose all stacks of Fury after this effect has resolved.
Harmonized
Your next Empower is performed with Advantage. Lose Harmonized after this effect has resolved.
Keen Eye
While performing an Attack, increase your Attack equal to the stacks of Keen Eye you possess. Lose all stacks of Keen Eye after this effect has resolved.
Regeneration
If Regeneration would be applied, add stacks of Regeneration equal to (Action ÷ 10). At the end of your Turn you must either expend 1 stack of Regeneration and restore 1 HP or expend 3 stacks of Regeneration and restore 1 Aura. If your HP and Aura are at their maximum values, expend 1 stack of Regeneration.
Reinforced
Armor can decrease Damage to 0 instead of a minimum of 1. Your next Persevere is performed with Advantage. Lose Reinforced when Damage is decreased to 0 from an Action or a Persevere Action is performed.
