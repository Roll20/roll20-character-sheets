Spiked (+1) :
? Emotion Spikes

When your emotion spike checkboxes are filled for any given emotional state, that emotion is considered "spiked" and becomes +1. All other states become -2.

You may keep using your spiked out emotion at the new value of +1. Each time you use it, mark a checkbox here.

Once you've used the spiked emotion five times, it returns to normal and you clear all the tickmarks.

Alternatively, you may use any other state at a one-time value of -2 and then clear all the tick marks on the spiked state.
